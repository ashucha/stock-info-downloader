<p><strong>Apple Inc.</strong></p><p><strong>Company Summary</strong></p><p><strong>Business:</strong> Apple designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories, and offers related services.</p><p><strong>Products:</strong></p><ul><li>iPhone: Lineup of smartphones based on Apple's iOS operating system</li><li>Mac: Lineup of personal computers based on macOS operating system</li><li>iPad: Lineup of multipurpose tablets based on iPadOS operating system</li><li>Wearables, Home, and Accessories: Smartwatches, wireless headphones, Apple TV media streaming device, and accessories.</li></ul><p><strong>Services:</strong></p><ul><li>Advertising</li><li>AppleCare</li><li>Cloud Services</li><li>Digital Content</li><li>Payment Services</li></ul><p><strong>Market:</strong></p><ul><li>Consumers, small and mid-sized businesses, education, enterprise, and government</li><li>Distribution through direct and indirect channels, including cellular network carriers, wholesalers, retailers, and resellers</li></ul><p><strong>Competition:</strong></p><ul><li>Aggressive competition with price competition and frequent product introductions</li><li>Imitation by competitors and intellectual property infringement</li><li>Importance of continuous innovation and customer satisfaction</li><li>Global supply chain creates component availability and price fluctuations</li></ul><p><strong>Risks:</strong></p><ul><li>Economic downturn or consumer confidence decline</li><li>Political events, trade disputes, and supply chain disruptions</li><li>Inability to maintain product and service competitiveness</li><li>Dependence on single or limited-source suppliers</li><li>Intellectual property infringement</li><li>Compliance with complex and changing laws and regulations</li><li>Environmental, social, and governance expectations</li></ul>

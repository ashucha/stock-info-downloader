<p><strong>Company Summary</strong></p><p>NESS is a designer, developer, manufacturer, seller, and lessor of high-performance fully electric vehicles and energy generation and storage systems, offering related services. They primarily sell their products directly to customers and continue to expand their customer-facing infrastructure through various channels, including vehicle service centers, mobile services, body shops, Supercharger stations, and Destination Chargers. The company emphasizes performance, attractive styling, and the safety of its users and workforce in its product design and manufacturing. They are also investing in developing full self-driving technology for enhanced safety. Additionally, NESS strives to lower ownership costs through continuous efforts to reduce manufacturing costs and by offering tailored financial and other services.</p><p>Their mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy, driven by their engineering expertise, vertically integrated business model, and focus on user experience.</p><p>The company operates in two reportable segments:</p><ol><li><strong>Automotive:</strong> Includes the design, development, manufacturing, sales, and leasing of high-performance fully electric vehicles as well as sales of automotive regulatory credits. Additionally, this segment incorporates services and other, encompassing non-warranty after-sales vehicle services, parts, sales of used vehicles, retail merchandise, paid Supercharging, and vehicle insurance revenue.</li><li><strong>Energy Generation and Storage:</strong> Encompasses the design, manufacture, installation, sales, and leasing of solar energy generation and energy storage products and related services, along with sales of solar energy systems incentives.</li></ol><p><strong>Products and Services</strong></p><p><strong>Automotive</strong></p><ul><li>Four consumer vehicles: Model

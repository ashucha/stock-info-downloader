<p><strong>Company Summary</strong></p><p>Microsoft is a technology company that empowers every person and organization by providing cloud and edge computing platforms, business applications, personal computing, and gaming experiences. With a mission to create local opportunity, growth, and impact, Microsoft invests in AI, data, security, and sustainability.</p><p><strong>Key Business Areas:</strong></p><ul><li><strong>Cloud and Edge Computing Platforms:</strong> Azure, the company's global cloud, offers hybrid consistency, developer productivity, AI capabilities, and enterprise-level security.</li><li><strong>Business Applications:</strong> Dynamics 365, LinkedIn, and Microsoft 365 provide innovative solutions for customer relationship management, talent management, and productivity.</li><li><strong>Personal Computing:</strong> Windows, Surface devices, and Xbox gaming create immersive experiences.</li><li><strong>Gaming:</strong> Xbox Game Pass, cloud gaming, and first-party content engage gamers and expand the platform.</li><li><strong>Search and News Advertising:</strong> Bing, Microsoft Edge, and News deliver personalized search, native, and display advertising.</li></ul><p><strong>Focus Areas:</strong></p><ul><li>Transforming the workplace and business processes.</li><li>Building and running cloud-based services.</li><li>Applying AI and ambient intelligence to drive insights and productivity.</li><li>Tackling security from an integrated, end-to-end perspective.</li><li>Inventing new gaming experiences.</li><li>Using Windows to fuel cloud business.</li></ul><p><strong>Corporate Social Responsibility:</strong></p><ul><li>Committed to sustainability and responsible sourcing.</li><li>Addressing racial injustice and inequity.</li><li>Investing in digital skills development.</li></ul><p><strong>Headcount:</strong></p><ul><li>Approximately 221,000 employees as of June 30, 2023.</li></ul>
